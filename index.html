Definition Of Streaming.

Streaming refers to a multimedia file such as audio and video that can be instantly played without the need to download it first. A media content, recorded or live, can be considered a streamed content as long as it can be played on any device via the internet even if you do not download it. It works through a continuous transmission of the media content from a server to the client. Now what is streaming and how does it work? Streaming is the continuous transmission of audio or video files from a server to a client. With streaming, the media file being played on the client device is stored remotely, and is transmitted a few seconds at a time over the Internet.

Characteristics of a Good Stream

In our research, we found several ways on how to have a good stream:

- Promote your videos and channel on social media. Show the world what you are up to.

- Encourage your viewers to like, share, and subscribe.

- Create Teasers

- Always announce your live streams to your viewers the day before.

- Create SEO titles (a list of results that appear as snippets) and descriptions.

- Restream Events. (allows you to upload pre-recorded videos and have them live streamed on your behalf to any of your connected destinations).

- Restream Analytics. (measures your live streaming performance by giving you insights on your stream across multiple platforms).

- Stream consistently.
